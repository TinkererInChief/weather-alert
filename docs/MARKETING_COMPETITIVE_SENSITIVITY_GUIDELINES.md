## ğŸ” COMPETITOR INTELLIGENCE RISK

### What Competitors Can Learn From Bad Messaging:

If we say: **"7 data sources including PTWC, JMA, USGS, DART..."**

Competitor learns:
1. Which sources to approach for partnerships
2. Our coverage strategy (multiple redundant sources)
3. How many sources they need to match us
4. Specific authorities to replicate our coverage

If we say: **"GPT-4o-mini for scenario generation"**

Competitor learns:
1. Exact model to use for similar features
2. Our AI strategy and vendor choices
3. Cost structure (they can estimate our OpenAI spend)
4. Technical implementation approach

### Better Approach:

Say: **"Advanced AI scenario generation with high accuracy"**

Competitor learns:
1. We have AI capabilities (obvious from feature)
2. Nothing about implementation
3. Nothing about vendors or models
4. Nothing they can directly replicate
